House Rules

Identity Verification

Valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhar Card preferred) is required for all guests upon check-in.

02

Guest Policy

Only registered guests whose details were provided during booking are permitted. Additional visitors require prior approval.

03

Peaceful Environment

Please maintain tranquility. Loud music, dancing inside, or quarreling is prohibited as per community guidelines. Quiet hours: 10 PM - 8 AM.

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in: 12:00 PM | Check-out: 11:00 PM next day. Early/late requests subject to availability.

05

No Smoking Indoors

Smoking is only permitted in designated outdoor areas.

Property Care

Please treat our cottage with care. Any damage will be charged accordingly.

A serene reservoir perfect for birdwatching, nature walks, and photography. Spot migratory birds and enjoy peaceful lakeside views, especially during sunrise and sunset.

Expansive grasslands ideal for nature enthusiasts and photographers. Experience the beauty of open landscapes, wildflowers, and unique biodiversity in this ecological gem.
